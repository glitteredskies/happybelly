About

Happy Belly ‘east meets west’ cuisine takes classic street foods from China, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Malaysia and Thailand – the noodles, soups, curries and delicious dumplings – and created an eclectic menu to pick and choose from to suit.Happy Belly brings big and fresh Asian flavours to New Zealand.

The Team

Meet our excited team behind Happy Belly. From left to right - John, Marco and Tim. While Marco runs the books and front of house, John and Tim are creating delicious plates of food in the kitchen.

Location

We are located on 4 Remuera Road, Newmarket, Auckland. You can reach us via public transport - train station is a 5min walk away, bus stop is a 3 min walk away. There are also plenty of parking facilities around Newmarket.
Our opening hours will be 11am-Late, Tuesday-Sunday.
